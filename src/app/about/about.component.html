Everybody's got their own criteria. Here are mine:

  • Front-to-back listenability: no skipping songs, the whole album works as a cohesive piece of art. You can put it on and know you will enjoy the next however-many-minutes

  • Timelessness. The above is true over thousands of plays and many years. They make you wish "they still made music like this."

  • Emotional Connection: these albums contain the songs that you can use as medicine. They get you through hard times and elevate good times. You go to a special place when you hear them.
